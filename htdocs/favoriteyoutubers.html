FAVORITE ANIME YOUTUBERS

Gigguk

"Gigguk, is a Thai-British YouTuber and podcaster who is known for his comedic rants and reviews on anime and otaku culture. He is affiliated with the Kadokawa-backed agency GeeXPlus" - Wikipedia. I love watching his hilarious and relatable content on Youtube. He has done numerous collaborations with other youtubers/streamers that I love to watch such as Mori Calliope, Lilypichu, The Anime Man, CDavgVA, and a couple more.

The Anime Man

"The Anime Man, is a Japanese Australian YouTuber, voice actor, songwriter, and podcaster. His videos focus on anime, manga, Japanese culture, language, and gaming. He is also known for his interviews with people in the Japanese entertainment industry, such as light novel authors, manga artists, and voice actors in anime" - Wikipedia. I love watching his videos because, like Gigguk, his videos are interesting and very entertaining. He collabs with people from different industries which makes his content diverse and not at all repetitive.

CDawgVA

"CDawgVA, is a Welsh Tokyo-based YouTuber and voice actor. He is affiliated with the Kadokawa-backed agency GeeXPlus" - Wikipedia. Him, along with Gigguk and The Anime Man, created Trash Taste, a weekly podcast where they talk about various topics, play games, or have specials, wherein they do challenges. His favorite anime is JoJo's Bizarre Adventure which is a fact that he has been known for in his community. His content is very funny, interesting, and diverse which causes me and millions of other people to laugh hysterically whenever watching his content.

Akidearest

"Akidearest, is a Filipino-American YouTuber. She is best known for discussing anime, manga, otaku culture and Japanese-related content on her YouTube channel" - Wikipedia. Akidearest and The Anime Man are both anime youtubers (or anitubers) and are in a relationship with each other. Her videos are neat, enjoyable, and interesting. Her collaborations are very funny and I enjoy watching her videos.
