What is the website all about?

This website shows my journey in anime. I will be sharing my experience in anime and how I got into it. It will be showing my favorite animes and showing some recommendations for each major genre. I will also introduce my favorite anime youtubers who I enjoy watching.

What is anime?

Anime is a combination of hand-drawn and digital animation that originates from Japan. In Japan and in Japanese, anime (a term derived from the English word animation) describes all animated works, regardless of style or origin. Anime is a diverse medium with distinctive production methods that have adapted in response to emergent technologies. It combines graphic art, characterization, cinematography, and other forms of imaginative and individualistic techniques.
