What is the website all about?

This website shows my journey in anime. I will share my experience in anime and how I got into it. It will show my favorite animes and recommend some as well for each major genre. I will also introduce my favorite anime youtubers who I love to watch.

What is anime?

Anime is hand-drawn and computer animation originating from Japan. In Japan and in Japanese, anime (a term derived from the English word animation) describes all animated works, regardless of style or origin. Anime is a diverse medium with distinctive production methods that have adapted in response to emergent technologies. It combines graphic art, characterization, cinematography, and other forms of imaginative and individualistic techniques.
